Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Thryvenex Holdings LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the LoopRoot mobile application ("LoopRoot" or the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy, especially given the sensitive and deeply personal nature of addiction recovery. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the App.

By downloading, installing, or using LoopRoot, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

2. Our Core Privacy Principle

LoopRoot is designed from the ground up to be a completely offline, private application. We do not collect, transmit, store, or process any personal data whatsoever. Your recovery journey is yours alone.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicit. LoopRoot does NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, or fingerprints)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Your recovery data, journal entries, or habit information
  • Your health data or biometric data
  • Any data from other apps on your device
  • Advertising identifiers or marketing data
  • Network activity, browsing history, or search queries

We have no servers, no databases, no analytics dashboards, and no way to access your data — even if legally compelled.

4. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All data you create within LoopRoot is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's Core Data framework and UserDefaults. This includes:

  • Habit profiles (name, program type, start date, goals, reasons)
  • Daily loop entries (morning plans, afternoon check-ins, evening reviews)
  • Journal and gratitude entries
  • Craving logs (triggers, intensity, tools used, outcomes)
  • Achievement unlocks and badge progress
  • Surge balance and vault shop purchases
  • If-Then plans and coaching plan progress
  • App preferences (theme, notification times, pet selection)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it using the Encrypted Backup feature.

5. Encrypted Backup Feature

LoopRoot offers an optional Encrypted Backup feature that allows you to export your data as an AES-256 encrypted file. Key points:

  • Encryption uses a passphrase you create — we never see or store it
  • The exported file is saved to a location you choose (Files app, AirDrop, email, etc.)
  • We have no access to exported backup files
  • If you lose your passphrase, the backup cannot be recovered by anyone, including us
  • You are solely responsible for the security and storage of your backup files

6. In-App Purchases

LoopRoot offers premium subscriptions through Apple's StoreKit 2 framework:

  • Monthly: $4.99/month
  • Yearly: $39.99/year
  • Lifetime: $99.99 (one-time)

All purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details. For information about how Apple handles purchase data, please review Apple's Privacy Policy.

7. Notifications

LoopRoot uses iOS local notifications only. These are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device by the iOS operating system. No notification data is sent to any server. Notification content can be redacted using the Stealth Mode feature for additional privacy.

8. Biometric Authentication

If you enable the Privacy Lock feature, LoopRoot uses Apple's LocalAuthentication framework to verify your identity via Face ID or Touch ID. Your biometric data is handled entirely by Apple's Secure Enclave on your device. LoopRoot never accesses, stores, or processes your actual biometric data.

9. Third-Party Libraries

LoopRoot includes the following open-source libraries, none of which collect or transmit data:

  • DGCharts — chart rendering (local only)
  • Lottie — animation rendering (local only)
  • Swift Collections — data structures (local only)

No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included in LoopRoot.

10. Crisis Helpline Information

LoopRoot displays crisis helpline phone numbers (such as SAMHSA, 988, Crisis Text Line, and others) as a public service. When you call or text these numbers, you are communicating directly with those organizations, not with us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. Please review their respective privacy policies.

11. Health Information Disclaimer

LoopRoot displays health milestone information (such as "Carbon monoxide eliminated after 24 hours") based on published medical research. This information is displayed locally and is not actual health data. LoopRoot does not access Apple HealthKit or any health-related APIs. The health milestones are informational only and do not constitute medical advice.

12. Children's Privacy

LoopRoot is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since LoopRoot collects no data at all, there is no mechanism by which children's data could be collected. However, the App's content relates to addiction recovery and is designed for adult users.

13. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can delete all app data by deleting the LoopRoot app from your device
  • Deleting the app permanently and irreversibly removes all associated data
  • We cannot recover deleted data because we never had access to it
  • We recommend using the Encrypted Backup feature before deleting the app if you wish to preserve your data

14. Data Security

Your data is protected by multiple layers of security:

  • All data remains on your device and is never transmitted
  • iOS device encryption protects data at rest
  • Optional biometric lock (Face ID/Touch ID) adds an additional layer
  • Encrypted Backup uses AES-256-GCM, a military-grade encryption standard
  • Stealth Mode can hide sensitive notification content

15. International Users

Because LoopRoot stores all data locally on your device and does not transmit data to any servers, there are no international data transfers. Your data remains subject to the laws of the jurisdiction where your device is physically located.

16. CCPA, GDPR, and Other Privacy Regulations

LoopRoot's zero-collection architecture means that privacy regulations regarding data collection, processing, and transfer are inherently satisfied. We have no data to collect, share, sell, or delete because we never collect it in the first place. We support the principles behind CCPA, GDPR, and similar regulations, and our design philosophy exceeds their requirements.

17. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of LoopRoot after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy. Since we have no way to contact you (we don't have your email or any contact information), we encourage you to review this policy periodically.
